Biriyani

Category: Italian

Time: 30 min

Ingredients:

  • 1. 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2. 1 cup yogurt
  • 3. 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 4. 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 5. 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 6. Salt to taste
  • 7. 3 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • 8. Fried onions, mint leaves, and coriander leaves for garnish
  • 9. 2 green chilies, slit
  • 10. 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes and drained

Instructions:

  • 1. In a bowl, combine the chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well, cover, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  • 2. In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add the soaked and drained rice, cardamom pods, cloves, cinnamon stick, and salt. Cook the rice until it is 70% done. Drain the rice and set aside.
  • 3. In a large, heavy-bottomed pot, heat ghee or oil over medium heat. Add the sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • 4. Add the marinated chicken, chopped tomatoes, green chilies, mint leaves, and coriander leaves. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • 5. Sprinkle biriyani masala over the chicken mixture (if using) and mix well.
  • 6. Layer the partially cooked rice over the chicken mixture. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the rice is fully cooked.
  • 7. Once done, gently fluff the biriyani with a fork to mix the rice and chicken.
  • 8. Garnish with fried onions, mint leaves, and coriander leaves.
  • 9. Serve hot with raita or salad. Enjoy your delicious chicken biriyani!

Burrata Pizza

Category: Italian

Time: 30 min

Ingredients:

  • 1. 2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 2. 1 1/2 cups warm water (110°F/45°C)
  • 3. 3 1/2 to 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4. 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 5. 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 6. 2 teaspoons salt
  • 7. 3 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • 8. 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 9. 2 green chilies, slit1 ball of burrata cheese (about 8 ounces)
  • 10.1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 11. 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 12. 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 13. Salt and pepper to taste
  • 14. Crushed red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions:

  • In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  • In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir until the dough comes together.
  • Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead for about 8-10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. If the dough is too sticky, add a little more flour.
  • Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-1.5 hours, or until doubled in size.
  • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to preheat as well.
  • Punch down the risen dough and divide it into two equal pieces (this recipe makes two 12-inch pizzas).
  • On a floured surface, roll out each piece of dough into a 12-inch circle.
  • Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza peel or baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  • Spread 1/2 cup of pizza sauce over each dough round, leaving a small border around the edges.
  • Evenly distribute the halved cherry tomatoes over the sauce.
  • If using a pizza stone, carefully transfer the pizza to the preheated stone using the pizza peel or baking sheet. If not using a stone, bake the pizza directly on the parchment-lined baking sheet.
  • Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the tomatoes are slightly roasted.
  • Remove the pizza from the oven and immediately place the burrata cheese in the center of each pizza. The heat from the pizza will begin to soften the burrata.
  • Drizzle with extra-virgin olive oil and sprinkle with salt, pepper, and crushed red pepper flakes if using.
  • Scatter fresh basil leaves over the top of the pizza.
  • Let the pizza cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  • Slice and enjoy your homemade burrata pizza!
